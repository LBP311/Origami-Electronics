Head

Step 1

Begin with the rectangular paper.

Step 2

Fold the paper in half from top to bottom. Then unfold the paper.

Step 3

Turn the paper 90 degrees.
Fold and unfold the paper in half as shown by the dotted lines.

Step 4

Using the bottom-half and top layer of paper.
Fold both of the bottom-most corners to the center-line fold.

Step 5

Using the same portion of paper from Step 4.
Fold the base of the paper along the center-line fold as shown by the dashed line.
The smaller triangles can also be used as reference for the fold.

Step 6

Flip the paper to its opposite side.

Step 7

Fold each corner towards the center-fold of the paper.
Ensure that each flap/fold/portion of paper is folded.

Step 8

Fold the paper in half from bottom to top.

Step 9

Make a cut about half-way of the current creation.
It does not have to be perfect, just do your best.

Step 10

Fold from one point of the creation to the end of the cut made. Then do the same for the other point
Flip the paper over and repeat.

Step 11

Bring the outermost corners together.

Last Step

You are finished!!!
