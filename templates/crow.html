Bird

Step 1

Fold and un-fold the paper along the dotted lines.

Step 2

Fold both ends of the paper to the center-fold line as indicated by the dotted lines.

Step 3

Again, fold both ends of the paper to the center-fold line as indicated by the dotted lines.

Step 4

Fold the diamond in half to make a triangle.

Step 5

Fold triangle flap in half.

Step 6

Fold along the dotted lines.
Each side of the top triangle will meet the bottom portion.

Step 7

Repeat Step 5 onto bottom triangle.

Step 8

Fold in half backwards.
As you fold the top and bottom triangles will begin to fold themselves. This will form the Crow's head.

Last Step

Flatten and you are finished!
