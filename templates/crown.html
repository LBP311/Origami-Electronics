Crown

Step 1

Fold and un-fold the paper along the dotted lines.

Step 2

Fold both ends of the paper to the center-fold line as indicated by the dotted lines.

Step 3

Fold the bottom triangle up to its base.

Step 4

Fold the lower triangle down in half.
The point should meet the lower center-point of the paper.

Step 5

Fold the base of the paper along the base of the smaller triangle.
The line to fold along is shown by a dashed line. Use the figure for reference.

Step 6

Fold the paper in half along the dotted line.
Then, unfold the paper.

Last Step

This is the first piece in creating the Crown!!!
Follow the previous steps as needed to create the necessary amount of units!!!
